Breaking Into Industry.

Breaking into a new industry can be a daunting task, and for many, it's the scariest part of pursuing a dream career. The uncertainty, competition, and sheer amount of unknowns can be overwhelming, leaving many feeling stuck and unsure of where to start.


One of the biggest challenges of getting into a new industry is the lack of experience and connections. Many industries require a certain level of expertise and networking, which can be difficult to acquire without prior experience. This creates a catch-22 situation where you're expected to have experience to get hired, but you can't get experience without being hired.

Moreover, the job market is constantly evolving, and industries are becoming increasingly specialized. This means that even if you have experience in one area, it may not be directly applicable to another. The stakes are high, and the pressure to succeed can be intense.
